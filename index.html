What We Do

Electrical Services Done Properly

From single power points to full home rewires — every job gets the same level of care and craft.

House Rewiring & New Wiring

Full and partial rewires for older Ballarat homes, plus new circuits for renovations and extensions. Every job is completed to Australian Standards with minimal disruption to your day.

Switchboard Upgrades

Replace outdated ceramic fuses with modern safety switches and circuit breakers to protect your family and appliances. Ideal for older Redan and Ballarat properties that haven't been touched in decades.

Lighting & Power Points

Indoor and outdoor lighting design, LED upgrades, downlights, and additional GPOs wherever you need them. Tidy installations with no holes left unfinished and no mess left behind.

Security Camera Installation

CCTV and security camera systems set up properly the first time, with cabling run cleanly and out of sight. Keep an eye on your home or business with gear that actually works when you need it.

General Repairs & Fault Finding

Tripping circuits, dead power points, dodgy switches — the niggling issues most sparkies don't want to chase. LPM tracks down the problem and fixes it properly, no shortcuts.
